Dwarf Planets

Dwarf planets are small celestial bodies that orbit the Sun and are nearly round in shape, but they have not cleared their orbits of other objects. Famous dwarf planets include Pluto, Eris, Haumea, Makemake, and Ceres. They are found in different regions of the Solar System and help scientists better understand its formation and evolution.
