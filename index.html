Solar System

The Solar System is a collection of the Sun, eight planets, their moons, and many other objects such as asteroids, comets, and dwarf planets. The Sun is at the center, and its gravity keeps everything in orbit. Each planet is unique, with different sizes, temperatures, and features, making the Solar System an exciting place to explore and learn about.
