Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. About 71% of its surface is covered by water, while the rest is made up of land. Earth has a breathable atmosphere that protects living things and helps maintain a suitable temperature. It has one natural satellite, the Moon, and takes 365 days to complete one orbit around the Sun.

Earth Facts

  • Type:Terrestrial
  • Diameter:12,742 km
  • Distance from Sun:149.6 million km
  • Day Length:24 hours
  • Year Length:356.25 days
  • Average Temperature:15°C
  • Gravity:9.81 m/s2
  • Number of Moons:1
  • Atmosphere:78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and traces of argon, carbon dioxide, and other gases
  • Fun Fact:Earth is the only known planet that supports life and has liquid water on its surface.
