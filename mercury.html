Solar System

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our Solar System and the closest planet to the Sun. It has a rocky surface filled with many craters caused by asteroid impacts. Mercury has no moons or rings, and a year on Mercury lasts only 88 Earth days. Because it has almost no atmosphere, temperatures change dramatically, becoming extremely hot during the day and very cold at night.

Mercury Facts

  • Type:Terrestrial
  • Diameter:4,879 km
  • Distance from Sun:57.9 million km
  • Day Length:58.6 Earth days
  • Year Length:88 Earth days
  • Average Temperature:167°C
  • Gravity:3.7 m/s2
  • Number of Moons:0
  • Atmosphere:Very thin exosphere made mostly of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium
  • Fun Fact:A single day on Mercury (sunrise to sunrise) lasts 176 Earth days, which is longer than its year (88 Earth days)!
